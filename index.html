What Scrap We Buy

We offer competitive pricing, swift on-site evaluation, and immediate logistical clearance for all bulk commercial and industrial scrap categories.

IT & Corporate Scrap

Complete e-waste disposal and asset recovery for IT companies, call centers, and corporate office parks.

  • Computers, Laptops & LCD Monitors
  • Servers, Routers & Network Racks
  • UPS Lead-Acid Batteries

AC Scrap & Chillers

Dismantling, recycling, and purchasing of all AC types (split AC, window AC, package units, and central chillers).

  • Split AC, Window AC & Package Units
  • Central Chillers & Cooling Towers
  • Outdoor AC Condensers & Compressors

Electric Cables & Wires

Buying copper and aluminium conductors with certified recovery and eco-friendly processing.

  • Armoured Copper Cables & wire scrap
